Your Action Plan

01

Audit Your Trash

For one week, don't just throw things away—look at them. Identify your biggest waste categories. Is it food packaging? Delivery boxes? Once you know the enemy, you can fight it.

02

Master Segregation

Implement a strict two-bin system at home: WET (organic, compostable) and DRY (recyclable plastics, paper, metal). Never mix the two. Contaminated recyclables end up in landfills.

03

Eliminate Single-Use

Stop buying bottled water. Carry a reusable coffee cup. Say no to plastic cutlery when ordering takeout. These tiny daily choices compound into massive environmental relief.

04

Start Composting

Up to 30% of household waste is organic. Put a compost bin on your balcony or in your yard. Turn your food scraps into nutrient-rich soil instead of landfill methane.
